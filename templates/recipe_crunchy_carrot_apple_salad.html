Crunchy Carrot & Apple Salad


Serves: 3 | Cooking Time: 15 mins


Ingredients:

2 tablespoons honey, 2 tablespoons walnut oil, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, 1 apple, 2 carrots, 1/2 cup walnut pieces


Method:

Firstly, core the apple and thinly sliced it. Peel the carrots and cut into coins and toast the walnuts for 5 mins. Secondly, in large bowl, whisk honey, oil and lemon juice.Toss apple and carrots with dressing. Lastly, sprinkle with walnuts into the salad bowl and serve immediately.
