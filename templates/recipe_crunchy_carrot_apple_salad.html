Crunchy Carrot & Apple Salad


Serves: 3 | Cooking Time: 15 mins


Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons walnut oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 apple
  • 2 carrots
  • 1/2 cup walnut pieces

Method:

  1. Firstly, core the apple and thinly sliced it. Peel the carrots and cut into coins.
  2. Toasted the walnuts for 5 mins.
  3. In large bowl, whisk honey, oil and lemon juice.
  4. Toss apple and carrots with dressing.
  5. Sprinkle with walnuts into the salad bowl and serve immediately.
