Introduction

myTrolley is an Web App that allows everyone and anyone, to borrow a trolley at a supermarket with just a click. This allows users who have access to the web to scan QR codes printed on the trolleys. Without the need for coins anymore, this method is faster, efficient, and also less time consuming. myTrolley is part of an initiative effort called Smart Nation, started by our Prime Minister, Lee Hsien Loong.


What is myTrolley's mission?

To provide convenient, affordable, and efficient ways to borrow trolleys from your local supermarket


Getting started

You have to register for an account if you don't have one yet. Look for the scanner option and allow the web app to use the camera. After that, simply scan the QR code on the handle of the trolley and start using it!


What makes myTrolley special?

myTrolley is one of the first trolley rental web application and the first and only one in Singapore! We thrive to improve whenever possible and maintain whatever neccesary.


Where does myTrolley operate?

myTrolley is currently only surpported by NTUC Fairprice. But 'fraid not! Soon myTrolley will be implemented throughout all local supermarkets in the country!


Other Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I rent a trolley?
A: Register for an account and your basic paymemt. Allow the web application to use the camera and scan the QR code on the handle of the trolley with the scanner and you are ready to go!
Q: How do I lock the trolley?
A: Park your trolley at the correct position, and press the "End trip" button to lock
Q: The scanner is not working, what should I do?
A: Go to settings and allow use of camera.
Q: How are the fares calculated?
A: Everytime you unlock a trolley, there will be a $1 deduction from your balance. When you finish using the trolley, end the trip to get the deposit back!
Q: For how long can I rent the trolley?
A: There is no time limit to the rental of trolleys. Feel free to use them as long as you want to!
Q: How far can I use the trolley?
A: There are indicators marked either within the vicinity of the supermarket or the shopping mall. If you push them out of the boundary, a timer will start counting down and your points may be deducted!
Q: What should I not do with the trolley?
A: You should not push the trolleys out of the indicated boundaries. Do not damage the trolleys in whatever way and treat the equipment with care.
Q: What should I do when i find a damaged/misplaced trolley?
A: Simply go to the scanner, and click on the "report" button below. Fill in the details and earn some points!
