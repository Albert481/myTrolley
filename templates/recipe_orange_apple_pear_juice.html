Orange, Apple & Pear Juice


Serves: 1-2 | Cooking Time: 5 mins


Ingredients:

  • 2 oranges
  • 1 red apple
  • 1 pear
  • ice cubes (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon clear honey (optional)

Method:

  1. Peel the oranges and divide the flesh into segments.
  2. Chop the apple and pear into even-sized pieces.
  3. Juice all the fruit in a blender.
  4. Pour the juice into a glass over ice.
  5. If using, stir in the honey and serve immediately.
