Orange, Apple & Pear Juice

Serves: 1-2 | Cooking Time: 5 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 oranges
  • 1 red apple
  • 1 pear
  • ice cubes (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon clear honey (optional)

Method:

  1. Peel the oranges and divide the flesh into segments.
  2. Chop the apple and pear into even-sized pieces.
  3. Juice all the fruit in a blender.
  4. Pour the juice into a glass over ice.
  5. If using, stir in the honey and serve immediately.

Creamy Banana Pudding

Serves: 1-2 | Cooking Time: 13 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2/3 cup banana, mashed (160g)
  • Icing sugar - 2 tbsps
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 3-4 tbsp coconut butter (45-60g)
  • 1 package tofu
  • 2-4 tbsp sugar of choice

Method:

  1. Make sure the coconut butter is melted before you begin.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a blender or food processor, and blend until completely smooth. Makes about 2 cups.
  3. Omit the tofu, and decrease the coconut butter to 2 tbsp.
  4. Decrease the salt to 1/8 tsp.
  5. Add 1 cup cashews that have been soaked in water 4-6 hours and drained fully.
  6. Vanilla stays the same, and sweeten as desired.
  7. Add milk of choice if a thinner pudding is desired.

Lettuce, Cucumber & Tomato Salad

Serves: 2 | Cooking Time: 10 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 lettuce
  • 2 cucumbers
  • 1 green onion
  • 1/4 cup Classic Vinaigrette
  • 1 pinch salt (optional)
  • 1 pinch ground pepper to taste (optional)

Method:

  1. Cut the tomatoes into segments and place them in a salad bowl.
  2. Add the lettuce leaves.
  3. Thinly slice the cucumbers and green onion tops.
  4. Then add the cucumbers and green onion tops to the bowl.
  5. Pour in the Classic Vinaigrette. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Toss and serve immediately.

Crunchy Carrot & Apple Salad

Serves: 3 | Cooking Time: 15 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons walnut oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 apple
  • 2 carrots
  • 1/2 cup walnut pieces

Method:

  1. Core the apple and thinly sliced it.
  2. Peel the carrots and cut into coins.
  3. Toasted the walnuts for 5 mins.
  4. In large bowl, whisk honey, oil and lemon juice.
  5. Toss apple and carrots with dressing.
  6. Sprinkle with walnuts into the salad bowl and serve immediately.
