Lettuce, Cucumber & Tomato Salad


Serves: 2 | Cooking Time: 10 mins


Ingredients:

  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 lettuce
  • 2 cucumbers
  • 1 green onion
  • 1/4 cup Classic Vinaigrette
  • 1 pinch salt (optional)
  • 1 pinch ground pepper to taste (optional)

Method:

  1. Cut the tomatoes into segments and place them in a salad bowl.
  2. Add the lettuce leaves.
  3. Thinly slice the cucumbers and green onion tops.
  4. Then add the cucumbers and green onion tops to the bowl.
  5. Pour in the Classic Vinaigrette. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Toss and serve immediately.
