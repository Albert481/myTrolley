Homemade Creamy Banana Pudding


Serves: 1-2 | Cooking Time: 13 mins


Ingredients:

2/3 cup banana (160g), 2 tbsps icing sugar, 1/4 tsp salt , 1 tsp pure vanilla extract, 3-4 tbsp coconut butter (50g), 1 package tofu, 2-4 tbsp sugar


Method:

Firstly, make sure the coconut butter is melted before you begin. You should combine all ingredients in a blender or food processor, and blend until completely smooth into 2 cups. Secondly, omit the tofu, and decrease the coconut butter to 2 tbsp and the salt to 1/8 tsp. Then, add 1 cup cashews that have been soaked in water 4-6 hours and drained fully. Vanilla stays the same, and sweeten as desired. Lastly, add milk of choice if a thinner pudding is desired and serve immediately.
